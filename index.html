Privacy

We Value Your Privacy

This hand-tracking paint app is designed to run directly in your browser. Your camera is used so the app can detect your hand, follow your fingertip, and let you draw on the screen. The camera feed is not uploaded by this app, not saved by this app, and not sent to a private server owned by this project. The hand tracking happens locally in the browser tab after the required model files load.

You stay in control of camera access. The app only starts the camera after you press the Start camera button and grant permission in your browser. When you press Stop camera, the app stops the active camera tracks so your browser can release the camera. Closing the tab also stops the session. If you ever feel unsure, you can use your browser's site settings to revoke camera permission for this page.

The drawing you create is kept in the page while the page is open. It is not automatically uploaded, synced, or attached to an account. Undo, redo, and clear canvas are handled in memory inside this tab. Refreshing or closing the page may remove the current drawing because the app is not trying to store your artwork silently.

The app uses MediaPipe Tasks Vision from a public CDN to load the hand-tracking library and model assets. Your browser may contact that CDN to download those files. After the files are loaded, the hand landmark detection runs in the browser. This project does not add analytics scripts, advertising trackers, account login, hidden recording, or background upload code.

Browser permissions are important. Modern browsers protect camera access by asking for permission and showing a camera indicator while the camera is active. If the page is opened as a local file, the browser may block camera access for security reasons. Running the app from localhost lets the browser apply normal camera permission rules while still keeping the experience on your own machine.

We believe a creative tool should be clear about what it does. The camera is used for hand tracking, the fingertip position is used to draw, and theme preferences are saved locally so the page can remember whether you prefer dark mode or light mode. We do not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, microphone, files, or any other permission that is not needed for this drawing experience.

If you share your screen, stream your browser, install browser extensions, or use third-party recording software, those tools may be able to see what appears on your screen. That is separate from this app. This app itself is built to keep the camera feed inside the browser session and to give you a visible Stop camera control whenever tracking is running.

In short: the camera is for live hand tracking, your drawing stays local to the page, the privacy-sensitive controls are visible, and you can stop the camera whenever you want.
